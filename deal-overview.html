What We Do

Real estate ownership, without the headaches of being a landlord

Tritower acquires single commercial properties — a pharmacy, a fast-food location, a medical office — and structures each one as its own REIT. Investors own a direct interest in a specific building with a specific tenant on a long-term NNN lease.

There's no blind pool. No wondering what's in the fund. Your clients know exactly what they own before they invest.

Tritower handles everything: acquisition, property management, tenant relationships, compliance, and reporting. Investors receive regular income distributions and a K-1 at year-end reflecting real estate depreciation.

How It Works

From introduction to invested in six steps

  • 1
    Advisor IntroductionTritower introduces a specific available property — tenant, location, lease terms, projected return.
  • 2
    Client Accreditation ReviewAdvisor confirms client qualifies as an accredited investor. Tritower provides all Reg D disclosure documents.
  • 3
    Subscription DocumentsClient reviews and executes the subscription agreement. Minimum investment typically $50,000–$100,000.
  • 4
    Capital Call & CloseFunds are called at closing. Investor is a REIT shareholder from day one.
  • 5
    Quarterly DistributionsIncome from NNN lease payments flows to investors quarterly.
  • 6
    Exit at Hold PeriodProperty is sold at the end of the defined hold period (typically 5–7 years). Net proceeds distributed.
